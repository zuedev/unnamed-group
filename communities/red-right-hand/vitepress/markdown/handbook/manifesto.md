# 174th Battle Group "Red Right Hand" Manifesto

**Motto:** Dextera Sanguinea Vindictae

## PREAMBLE: The Necessity of the Shadow

Civilization is built by the Left Hand. The Left Hand writes laws, signs treaties, shakes hands, and builds walls to keep the darkness out. But the Left Hand is bound by the very rules it creates. It is slow. It is public. It is merciful.

But there are threats that do not respect laws, treaties, or walls. There are evils that hide in the blind spots of justice. When the Left Hand is tied, the Right Hand must act.

## I. WE ARE THE CORRECTION

The world is an equation that constantly unbalances. Terror, anarchy, and corruption seek to tip the scales toward chaos. We are the weight on the other side. We do not seek war, but we finish it. We are the red ink on the ledger of history, balancing the debt of blood with blood.

## II. WE ARE THE INSTRUMENT, NOT THE MASTER

A hand does not think; it acts according to the will of the body. We have no politics. We have no personal vendettas. We are the kinetic manifestation of the State’s will. Where the State points, we strike. We are not judges; we are the executioners.

## III. THE DOCTRINE OF TOTALITY

There is no half-measure in survival. When the Red Right Hand is deployed, the objective is not negotiation or containment. The objective is erasure. We do not wound; we excise. We paint the final line so that others may live within the margins of safety.

## IV. THE PRICE OF SILENCE

We walk in the dark so that the light remains untainted by our deeds. We accept that our victories will never be celebrated, our sacrifices never mourned, and our names never known. We are the sins of the State, buried deep, so the public may sleep with a clear conscience.

**We are the long reach of justice and the bloody hand of fate.**
